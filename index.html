e- Academies

e-Academy

Virtual Net. Real Results.

For Players: Our platform connects talented players to cricket academies across the world. An ever-growing number of academies have partnered with us to offer integrated hybrid (online and on-pitch) coaching modules, giving the best of both worlds to players like you.

For Academies: Our e-Academy model enables academies to engage with a larger audience as well as take their academy operations online, simplifying tasks such as managing coaches and students, scheduling training sessions (online or in-person), etc. You can also get special conditions to live-stream academy tournaments!
